Type A

One of the community’s most sought after villas, V 4A is a stunning four bedroom graciously sized residence. Reflecting the charm of a cosy Spanish cottage, the façade and the terracotta-tiled roof of the villas evokes the nostalgia of the unhurried Mediterranean life. Truly the best of both worlds, freshly built yet classically designed.
